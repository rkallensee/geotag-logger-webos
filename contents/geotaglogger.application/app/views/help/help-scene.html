Geotag logger 0.0.1
Record your position to geo-tag photos from an external camera.
Help
This application was designed to help with geo-tagging images from an external camera, e.g. your DSLR camera. The pictures from your WebOS phone can be easily geo-tagged by turning on the option in the phone settings. But for photo cameras without a GPS sensor, it's a bit more complicated. There are external devices you can buy to track your position and afterwards "merge" the geo-coordinates into your pictures. But since you own a WebOS device with a GPS sensor, you can just use it to log your position with this tiny application. Afterwards you can use GPicSync or one of the many other software to include the geo-information from the GPX file you exported from this application into your pictures.
